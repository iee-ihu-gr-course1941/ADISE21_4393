How To Play

The game is explained in four easy steps...

Game Start

A card is hidden and the rest are shared between the players.

Make Pairs

You have to make pairs with similar cards and toss them to the discard pile.

Steal A Card

Each player draws a card from the player on the left in order to make new pairs.

Lose Or Get Drawn

The player with the unpaired card is the loser and the one who is painted by the Old Maid.
